Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Share Market? +
The share market is a platform where investors buy and sell shares of publicly listed companies to earn profit.
Who can learn Share Market Trading? +
Anyone above 18 years with basic financial knowledge can learn share market trading, including students and working professionals.
Do I need prior experience to start trading? +
No prior experience is required. Our courses start from beginner level and move towards advanced strategies.
What is Intraday Trading? +
Intraday trading involves buying and selling stocks within the same trading day to earn short-term profits.
What is Long-Term Investment? +
Long-term investment means holding stocks for several years to benefit from company growth and compounding.
What is Technical Analysis? +
Technical analysis studies price charts, patterns, and indicators to predict future price movements.
Is Share Market Trading risky? +
Yes, trading involves risk, but proper knowledge, risk management, and discipline can reduce losses significantly.
Do you provide live market training? +
Yes, we provide live market sessions where students learn real-time market analysis and trading execution.
Are offline classes available? +
Yes, offline classes are available at Nashik, Niphad, and Yeola branches.
How can I contact or enroll? +
You can contact us via WhatsApp, phone call, or by filling the inquiry form on our website.
